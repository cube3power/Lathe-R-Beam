ZLathe

Instructions

Click three or more times in the left window to create a profile shape. Try to make the first and last points as close to the dotted line as possible. When you're done, click 'Lathe' and see your creation turned into a 3D shape!

You can pan and tilt the 3D shape by moving your mouse, or export to STL format for 3D printing. Running through netfabb before attempting to print is highly recommended.
